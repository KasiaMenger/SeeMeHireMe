Project See me Hire me

This initiative is created for proffesional women, who are looking to get their careers back on track, however due to personal reasons, long maternity break or other circumstances are experiencing difficulties to find employment.

Here you can tell everyone who you are and what your strengths are.

  • Be honest
  • Be specific
  • Be yourself

    • Let everyone hear your story from when you were building your career path and why you decided to change direction or chose to stop.
    “It takes strength and courage to admit the truth.”
    Tell everyone why you want your career back and why you deserve a chance to be seen and hired again.

    “Don't let your dreams be dreams.”

    What to talk about in your video
      Here's few tips:
    • Treat is as if it was an interview.
      • The goal of any interview is to determine whether the candidate will be outstanding in the job, and that means evaluating the skills and attitude required for that job. Talk about why you took certain jobs. Explain why you left. Explain why you chose a certain school. Discuss why you took time off, and what you got out of the experience.
    • Mention your biggest strengths.
      • Be clear and precise. Provide sharp, on-point examples of what are your greatest attributes. Don't just claim to have certain attributes - prove you have them.
    • Describe your passions, desires and commitment.
      • Talk about how the position you want to be hired for is a perfect fit for what you want to accomplish.
    • And finally make sure to fit all that in the 2 minutes video frame.
      • Good Luck!
